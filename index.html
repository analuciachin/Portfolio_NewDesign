About me

Hi, there! I am Ana Lucia, a Lighthouse Labs Web Developer Bootcamp student. Born in Brazil and living in Canada. I am a data analyst transitioning to become a front-end developer.

Working as a data analyst, I have developed analytical and problem solving skills, logical thinking and a great understanding of SQL and databases as I have been working in the field for over 10 years.

In my current job, I have developed a web application to help the Fraud Team look for suspicious credit card transactions and flag high risk customers. The application was built using HTML, Bootstrap and Python (with Flask framework).

I also have a huge interest in UX/UI design and slowly I would like to move towards becoming a front-end developer with a solid knowledge in UX/UI.

Right now I am participating in the #100DaysOfCode challenge where I have committed to code everyday for 100 days and posting my daily work on Twitter.
